Our Projects

Cosmo Salon, along with its associated Cosmo Academy of Hair and Cosmetology by Aadi Rana, offers various services and courses related to the beauty and fashion industry. Here’s a breakdown of the information provided:

Cosmo Films: Cosmo Salon presents Cosmo Films, a service that aims to freeze and capture valuable moments for customers. They specialize in wedding, commercial, product, and portfolio photography and videography. Their style focuses on photography and fashion filmmaking, aiming to capture moments and candid shots with natural or artificially created lighting, ensuring a high level of perfection. Cosmo Academy of Hair and Cosmetology by Aadi Rana: Cosmo Salon has launched the Cosmo Academy of Hair and Cosmetology by Aadi Rana. This academy aims to provide a platform for individuals to enhance their capabilities in the field of cosmetology and become expert stylists through advanced international-level training. The academy’s curriculum is designed to prepare students for the international beauty industry. The training programs incorporate technical, personal, and business skills necessary to succeed in the hair and beauty industry.

Hair sculpting and design: This course focuses on teaching the art of sculpting and designing hair, providing students with the necessary skills and techniques to create various hairstyles and designs.
